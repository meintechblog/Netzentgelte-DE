# Commercial Licensing

This project is primarily distributed under the **Energy Community License (ECL-1.0)**.

The goal of this license is to support the energy community while preventing
commercial reselling of the software.

However, commercial licenses are available.

---

## When you need a commercial license

You must request a commercial license if you want to:

* sell the software as a product
* sell hardware devices with the software preinstalled
* bundle the software in commercial energy products
* offer paid SaaS services based on this software
* integrate the software into a proprietary commercial system

---

## Typical commercial use cases

Examples where a commercial license may be required:

* energy companies
* inverter manufacturers
* energy management system vendors
* industrial hardware vendors
* SaaS providers

---

## Requesting a commercial license

If your company would like to use this software commercially,
please contact the author.

Commercial licenses can include:

* redistribution rights
* hardware bundling rights
* SaaS rights
* OEM integration rights
